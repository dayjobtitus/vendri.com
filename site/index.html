What We Do

We provide web and app publishers with modern tools for instant contextual display of media from any source on any screen.


Who We Are

Vendri is a no-code-required Actionable Data Platform for anyone who uses the internet to display some form of content on any platform. Make custom creatives and campaigns with media and ads of any type without worrying about how to display them, and control everything from brand protection, to monetization, to user experience.

Our Great Features:

Publisher Control

Stop loading tons of SDK's and paying for many services and still having to engineer how they all work together. One platform, all content, all ads, all partners, for all of your apps and websites.

Easy Integration

Integrating Vendri all the way to production, done in minutes.

No Server Reliance

Vendri ensures no slow cloud processing, no pre-caching, and no wasted bandwidth.

Enhanced Experience

No More Spinner: Vendri makes decisions on content and automates pulling from the correct sources and will not pre-cache or download anything unless it can be displayed.

Hyper-threaded technology

Using the power of every connected device, do more with the same internet.

Live Actionable Data

Beyond simply providing correlated data from all sources, Vendri enables publishers to take action on that data, prompting them to make better decisions about their content, in real time.

No Video/Audio Player Needed

With full automatic support for all asset types, no need to limit or add anything else. Use your player or let Vendri create one whenever it is needed.

Even Ads

Removing the barrier between ads and content, Vendri supports all ads from any partner you chose. We even guarantee viewability and provide you the control over campaigning, bidding, and fallbacks.

Speed-up. Control. Monetize.

With over 10x improvement on performance across all customer platforms today, our publishers are now able to apply more contextual controls around their content and even monetize in new ways. Supporting all media types and ad technologies such as VAST and VPAID, there are no restrictions.
