Privacy Policy

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practice for www.vendri.com. This privacy policy applies sole to information collected by this website. It will notify you of the following:

  • What personally identifiable information is collected from you through the website, how it is used and with whom it may be shared
  • What choices are available to you regarding the use of your data.
  • The security procedures in place to protect the misuse of your information.
  • How you can correct any inaccuracies in the information.

    • Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

    Vendri inc. is the sole owners of the information collected on this site. We only have access to/collect information that you voluntarily give us via email or other direct contact from you. Vendri inc. will not sell or rent this information to anyone. Vendri inc. will use your information to respond to you, regarding the reason you contacted us. Vendri inc. will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill your request, e.g. to ship an order. Unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about specials, new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy.

    Your Access to and Control Over Information

    You may opt out of any future contacts from us at any time. You can do the following at any time by contacting Vendri inc. via the email address or phone number given on our website:

  • See what data we have about you, if any.
  • Change/correct any data we have about you.
  • Have us delete any data we have about you.
  • Express any concern you have about our use of your data.

    • Security

    Vendri inc. takes precautions to protect you information. When you submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both on/offline. Wherever Vendri in. collects sensitive information (such as credit card data), that information is never done through the website, but through encrypted contact with our billing department. While we use encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted online, we also protect your information offline. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, billing or customer service) are granted access to personally identifiable information are kept in a secure environment.

    Updates

    Vendri inc.’s Privacy Policy may change from time to time and all updates will be posted on this page, including an email stating that a change in the “Privacy Policy” has occurred.
